> _"Feeling like you're clueless isn't a bug, it's a system requirement for enhanced efficiency and security"_

I am a developer driven by **insatiable curiosity**.
My journey began with modding games and managing servers, evolving into a passion for full-stack architecture.
I treat every "404: Knowledge Not Found" as an invitation to explore.
I embrace the "I'm feeling dumb" vibes, roughly translated to: **"Level Up in Progress"**.

I'm mostly self-taught, I learned by having early access to PCs and a lifetime of problems to pull apart.
I pick things up fast, go deep when something matters, and tend to map edge cases other people skip.
For me, every path is worth understanding, filtering through the practical question of whether the resources fit the goal.
